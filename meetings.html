Our meetings are intended for anyone who have or have had cancer, any kind of cancer. It is not intended for caregivers.

We DO NOT offer Professional Diagnoses or Treatments. Our mission is to provide support, not to diagnose or prescribe. If you need medical support, please consult your healthcare provider.
We meet once a month for to share strength, resilience, and hope. Whether you come to share, to listen, or to just be in the presence of others who understand, Waves of Resilience welcomes you.

Our meetings will be on the third Friday of the month, starting on January 17th at 2:00pm at the Southwest Neighborhood Library, Room A, 7979 38th Avenue, Kenosha
